Project Blog


In the summer of 2021, I worked under the guidance of my mentors Simon, Rick and Ricardo to add Out-of-band Application Security Testing (OAST) Support to ZAP, the world's most widely used web app scanner. Since then, I have continued to work on enhancing the OAST add-on for ZAP. Read more about ZAP at zaproxy.org.

Follow @zaproxy on Twitter for latest ZAP updates.
